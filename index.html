About George The Tutor

We're a team of experienced IB educators and examiners dedicated to helping students succeed. With a passion for education and a proven track record, we provide customized tutoring that addresses individual learning styles.

Our mission: To empower students with the tools, strategies, and confidence to excel in the IBDP and beyond. Our values: Integrity, Expertise, and Personalized Attention.

Our tutors undergo rigorous training and maintain up-to-date knowledge of the IBDP curriculum. We continuously refine our approaches based on student feedback, ensuring that our methods remain relevant and effective.

Curious about how we operate? Here are some common inquiries.

Q: What subjects do you cover?

A: We offer tutoring across all major IBDP subjects including Sciences, Mathematics, Languages, Humanities, and TOK.

Q: How do I schedule a session?

A: Simply contact us with your preferred dates and subjects. We'll match you with the right tutor and arrange a convenient time.

A: Yes, we offer a free initial consultation to understand your needs and show how we can help.

Q: Can I switch tutors if needed?

A: Absolutely. We prioritize your comfort and learning experience. If a change is needed, just let us know.

Q: How experienced are your tutors?
